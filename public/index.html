Regulate, Play, Thrive!

Survive.com is an app to help kids, teens, and parents regulate online time. It turns healthy offline activities—like chores, family time, and personal tasks—into missions that earn XP. XP can be redeemed for rewards, including potential real-world allowance, based on household rules. Missions encourage real-life skills: chores, fitness, sleep, creativity, mental health, STEM, volunteering, career, money, relationships, faith, and more!

Unique Family & Friend Games
  • 🕹️ Mission Race: Compete to complete the most real-life missions in a day. Track XP, unlock fun rewards!
  • 🎨 Offline Challenge: Draw, cook, or build something creative and share a photo in the Vaulted Chat.
  • 📷 Photo Hunt: Snap photos of things you accomplished (chores, projects, hikes)—parents and teens vote for favorites.
  • 💬 Vaulted Stories: Write and share a story or poem in the Vaulted Chat (parents/child/friends).
  • 🤔 Starter Showdown: Take turns answering daily family starter questions. Earn XP for best answers.
  • 🌟 XP Board: Play together, see who levels up fastest offline & online (connect as a group, compare XP stats).
Play these games with your family or friends, online or offline, and build deeper connections!
